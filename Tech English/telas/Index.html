Business @ The Speed of Thought

Por Bill Gates

Business @ the Speed of Thought explores how digital technology can transform the way businesses operate. Bill Gates emphasizes the importance of integrating information technology into every aspect of a company, from decision-making to customer relations. By leveraging real-time data, businesses can increase efficiency, respond faster to market changes, and innovate continuously. The book combines practical advice with Gates’ vision of a digitally connected business world.
